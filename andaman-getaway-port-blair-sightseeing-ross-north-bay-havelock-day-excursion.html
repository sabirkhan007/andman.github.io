Tour Plan
  • Day 1: Port Blair: Corbyn’s Cove Beach, Cellular Jail - Light And Sound Show

    Upon arrival at Port Blair, our team member will receive you and assist in boarding a cab to the appointed hotel. Check-in and freshen up whereafter our team will take you to Corbyn’s Cove Beach some 20 minutes’ drive.

    Corbyn’s Cove beach is renowned for the exotic view of the coconut and palm-fringed beachline and turquoise blue sea, an ideal place for sunbathing and water sports activities from jet skiing to boating, surfing and swimming. It is a good place to relax after a long flight.

    Later in the evening, we travel to Cellular jail (KALA PANI) the colonial-era prison a standing landmark witness to the horrors and atrocities meted out to freedom fighters. The enthralling Light and Sound show at this historic site throws light on how freedom fighters fought or diminished into history.

    After the jail tour, our cabs take you to your resort. Overnight at Port Blair.

  • Day 2: (Port blair)North Bay (Coral Island) – Ross Island - Port Blair

    After breakfast early,our team takes you to Water sports complex.From here we start for a Full-day boat excursion to North Bay (Coral Island) & Ross Island.

    First we head to North bay island to see coral reefs,exotic varieties of beautiful ornamental fishes and other marine life.You can enjoy glass bottom boat ride,snorkeling and other water sports activities like Jet Ski Ride, Sea walk etc (Optional).

    In the afternoon,we visit Ross Island, from here the British governed the entire Andaman & Nicobar Islands,prior to India’s Independence.There is also a cemetery and a small museum managed by the Indian Navy.This Island is an ideal destination for nature walk amidst sylvan surroundings with deer, peacocks, exotic birds, etc.

    After exploring ross island, we head back to Port Blair.Upon arrival our cabs take you back to your resort. Overnight stay at Port Blair.

  • Day 3: (Port blair)Havelock – Radhanagar Beach –SAME DAY RETURN

    After breakfast and checkout ,our cab take you to the jetty,from here you board the inter-island cruise to Havelock Island (57 kms by sea from Port Blair).

    On Arrival at Havelock Jetty, our team will assist you board the AC Cab waiting to transfer you to the World Famous “Radha Nagar Beach”
    Enjoy afternoon at Radhanagar Beach,situated on the western coast of Havelock Island,it is one of the most popular destinations for tourists in Andaman Islands. With its breathtaking sunsets,this beach is nothing less but a tropical heaven.Pristine sand,and the clear emerald blue waves and a lot of peace and serenity are the hallmark of this beautiful paradise.

    In the Evening,our team will take you back to Havelock jetty and assist you board your cruise back to Port Blair.Overnight stay at Port Blair.

  • Day 4: (Port blair)Departure

    After breakfast and checkout,our cab will be waiting outside to take you to the Airport .

    .....Tour Ends with Memorable Journey.....
