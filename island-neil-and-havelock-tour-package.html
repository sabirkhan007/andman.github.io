Tour Plan
  • Day 1: Port Blair: Corbyn’s Cove Beach, Cellular jail - Light and Sound Show

    Upon arrival at Port Blair, our team member will receive you and assist in boarding a cab to the appointed hotel. Check-in and freshen up whereafter our team will take you to Corbyn’s Cove Beach some 20 minutes drive.

    Corbyn’s Cove beach is renowned for the exotic view of the coconut and palm-fringed beachline and turquoise blue sea, an ideal place for sunbathing and water sports activities from jet skiing to boating, surfing, and swimming. It is a good place to relax after a long flight.

    Later in the evening, we travel to Cellular jail (KALA PANI) the colonial-era prison a standing landmark witness to the horrors and atrocities meted out to freedom fighters. The enthralling Light and Sound show at this historic site throws light on how freedom fighters fought or diminished into history.

    After this, our cabs drop you at your resort. Overnight at Port Blair.

  • Day 2: Baratang - Rangat

    Before the day breaks, we will make progress to Baratang Island, which is around 110 km from Port Blair. Driving through the Andaman trunk road you will stop briefly at the Jikratng police check post and proceed further in convoys through the Jarawa Reserve Forest said to be home to the Jarawa tribe. We will ride through the diverse tropical rainforest region, where you can enjoy the beauty and the biodiversity of nature. After passing through the tribal reserve, you would reach Nilambur Jetty. You would then be transferred to a vehicle ferry, which would take you to Baratang Island. These ferries can accommodate buses, trucks, cars & bikes, and passengers too, to cross the waters between Nilambur Jetty at Middle Strait and Baratang.

    Upon reaching Baratang board a smaller boat that will take you to the limestone cave site a 25 minutes boat ride through the unique mangrove trees creeks on both sides creating a natural passage.

    The naturally formed limestone caves at Nayadera and the vast mangrove-covered creeks are major attractions for tourists in Baratang. A few moments later get back into your boats back to Baratang. Board our cabs and proceed to Rangat for lunch. On the way, we will stop at Amkunj Beach for some time before we move to our resort for check-in. Overnight stay at Rangat.

  • Day 3: Rangat - Diglipur

    After breakfast and checkout board our cabs. First, we visit Dhani Nallah beach in the morning today. Walk through Mangrove creek to reach this secluded Beach. Enjoy for a couple of hours, then we proceed further towards Diglipur.In the afternoon, we stop at Mayabunder and head to Kramatang beach for a couple of hours.

    In the evening, we leave for our final destination for the day, Diglipur checks in to your resort. Overnight at Diglipur.

  • Day 4: Diglipur- Ross & Smith Island

    After breakfast board our cabs and head to Aerial Bay jetty, some 15 minutes’ drive. The day is dedicated to Ross and Smith Island. Here a small boat will take you to Ross & Smith Island.

    Ross & Smith islands are two islands – Ross & Smith joined together by a sand bar to make a single group of islands. What makes these islands truly beautiful and sets them apart from the rest of the islands is that these two islands are joined by a natural, sand bar. With the sea on both sides. Water is crystal clear and gem green in color. Sunbathing, Swimming, and Snorkeling in Ross & Smith Islands is a wonderful experience. You will see a lot of beautiful fish underwater. During high tide, the sand bar disappears leaving a trail of water between the two islands, and during low tide, the sand bar appears again making the islands look majestic! There is a Marine Sanctuary on the island which makes it an ideal place for spotting beautiful coral reefs and colorful species from underwater.

    Enjoy your day at this magical destination. In the afternoon/evening board the boat to head back to Aerial Bay Jetty. Once back our cabs will take you back to your resort. Relax at your resort. Spend some time at Kalipur beach in the evening, which is at a 5 minutes’ walk from your resort.

  • Day 5: Diglipur- Mud Volcano and Ram Nagar Beach

    After breakfast, board our cabs and head to the famous Mud Volcanos of North Andaman.

    Located near Hathilevel at a distance of 20 km from Diglipur a chain of Mud volcanoes can be witnessed in the green jungle of Shyamnagar at Diglipur a brisk 15-minute walk in the semi-evergreen forests. First, we find a big and dormant mud volcano but a little distance away oozing mud volcanos are seen. Seating facilities along with information boards are provided for visitors.

    Time for lunch at Diglipur. After lunch gets in cabs and head to the Famous Ram Nagar Beach. It is less popular but an amazing beach with a thick tropical forest reaching out to the shores. One can site the highest mountain peak of Andaman and Nicobar Island called the Saddle Peak. In the evening we drive back to Rangat. Overnight stay at Rangat.

  • Day 6: Rangat -Long Island

    Today after breakfast and checkout, our team will take you to Yarrata Jetty which is about a 40-minute drive from the resort at Rangat. From here we board the cruise to Long Island (9 AM).

    On reaching Long Island Jetty, head to your resort for check-in. The rest of the day is for you to relax and unwind.

  • Day 7: Long Island

    The whole day is for you to enjoy this secluded paradise, you can take a small boat ride to visit nearby Islands like Guitar Island, North passage island, and Strait Island. (Optional)

  • Day 8: Long Island - Rangat - Port Blair

    Today we start early after breakfast and head back to Rangat. Once you reach Yarrata Jetty, board the cabs and head to Port Blair, a four-hour drive, about 160 km. On reaching Port Blair check in to your hotel. The rest of the evening is for you to relax after a long drive, or to explore on your own.

  • Day 9: Port Blair - Havelock - Radhanagar Beach

    After breakfast and checkout, our team member will take you to Haddo jetty by Cab, and board the inter-island cruise to Havelock Island (57 km by sea from Port Blair).

    Havelock is a picturesque natural paradise with beautiful white sandy beaches, rich coral reefs, and lush green forests. It is one of the most populated islands in the Andaman group of islands, with an area of 113 sq. km. On Arrival at Havelock Jetty, our team will assist you to board the AC Cab waiting to transfer you to the hotel.

    After lunch, board our Cabs and visit the World Famous “Radha Nagar Beach” situated on the western coast of Havelock Island, it is one of the most popular destinations for tourists in the Andaman Islands. With its breathtaking sunsets, this beach is nothing less than tropical heaven. Pristine sand, clear emerald blue waves and a lot of peace and serenity are the hallmarks of this beautiful paradise. Witness the famous Sunset from Radhanagar beach and head back to your resort. Overnight at Havelock.

  • Day 10: Havelock - Kalapathar beach & Elephanta Beach

    After breakfast, we head to Elephanta Beach, the ancient secluded beach. Elephanta beach is famous for all kinds of water sports. The rest of the day is dedicated to your love of the ocean and associated water sports like scuba, snorkeling, parasailing, etc.

    Get charmed by the sugary white sand and the contrasting shadows of the old trees at the beach, which creates an illusionistic picturesque to mesmerize you. Spend the day snorkeling in the water and take the exotic view of the corals and the marine life under the sea, at Elephanta beach.

    In the evening, we proceed to Kala Pathar Beach. The beach gets its name from the surrounding village and the black rocks that adorn the coastline here. Silvery sand and aqua green waters make the place exotic and exquisite and make this beach a great place to relax. It is a long stretch of white silky sand with big black rocks beside the blue sea and it is situated on one corner of Havelock Island.

    In the evening we return to our resort. Overnight stay at Havelock.

  • Day 11: Havelock – Neil Island

    After breakfast, our team will take you to Havelock jetty and help you board the cruise which will sail to Neil Island.

    Neil is a tiny but beautiful Island located 37 km to the south of the Andaman Islands with unexplored coral reefs, brilliant bio-diversity, white sandy beaches, and tropical forest and vegetation.

    On arrival at Neil Island, our team will receive and escort you to your resort. After lunch, board our cabs and head to Bharatpur Beach, which is ideal for water sports like scuba, snorkeling, and glass bottom boat rides. You can enjoy the rest of the evening here, then return to your resort. Overnight stay at Neil Island.

  • Day 12: Neil - Sightseeing

    After breakfast visit Lakshmanpur beach, where you can see a natural bridge formation also known as Howrah Bridge.

    The whole day is dedicated to the tropical outdoors. In the evening, our team member will escort you back to your resort. Overnight stay at Neil.

  • Day 13: Neil Island – Port Blair

    Check out from the hotel after breakfast and visit Sitapur beach, in the afternoon or evening (depending on your cruise schedule), our representative will escort you to the jetty area, help you board your cruise and return to Port Blair.

    On arrival at Port Blair, our team will take you to your resort and help you check in. Overnight stay at Port Blair.

  • Day 14: Departure

    After breakfast, checkout from the hotel, and our cab will be waiting outside to take you to the Airport.
