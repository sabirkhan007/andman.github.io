Note- In the Andaman Islands, cab services are known to have great demand and there is a great shortage of vehicles. So, it is recommended to book your cabs in advance to avoid any hassle in the last moments. When choosing cab services, be watchful because you may not be able to call the cab person due to the poor connectivity of the internet and mobile networks across the islands. Hence, app-based taxi services are no longer available in the Andaman Islands, as of 2020.
