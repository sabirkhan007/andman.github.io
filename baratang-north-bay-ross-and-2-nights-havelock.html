Tour Plan
  • Day 1: Port Blair: Carbyn’s Cove Beach, Cellular jail -Light and Sound Show

    Upon arrival at Port Blair our team member will receive you and assist in boarding a cab to appointed hotel. Check in and freshen up whereafter our team will take you to Corbyn’s Cove Beach some 20 minutes’ drive.

    Corbyn’s Cove beach is renowned for the exotic view of the coconut and palm-fringed beachline and turquoise blue sea, an ideal place for sunbathing and water sports activities from jet skiing to boating, surfing and swimming. It is a good place to relax after a long flight.

    Later in the evening, we travel to Cellular jail (KALA PANI) the colonial-era prison a standing landmark witness to the horrors and atrocities meted out to freedom fighters. The enthralling Light and Sound show at this historic site throws light on how freedom fighters fought or diminished into history.

    After the jail tour, our cabs take you to your resort. Overnight at Port Blair.

  • Day 2: Port Blair - Full-day Excursion to Baratang - Limestone Cave, Jarwa Tribal reserve.

    Before the day breaks, we will make progress to Baratang Island, which is around 110 km from Port Blair. On the way, driving on Andaman trunk road, you will pass through the Jarawas Tribe Reserve. We will ride through the diverse forest region, where you can enjoy the beauty and the biodiversity of nature.After passing through the tribal reserve you would reach Nilambur Jetty. You would then be transferred to a vehicle ferry, which would take you to Baratang Island. These ferries can accommodate buses, trucks, cars & bikes and the passengers too, to cross the waters between Nilambur Jetty at Middle Strait and Baratang.

    The Limestone cave formations at Nayadera and the vast mangrove-covered creeks are the main attractions for tourists in Baratang.

    Upon reaching Baratang Board a smaller boat that will take you to the limestone cave site, during the 25 minutes boat ride, you will pass through the unique mangrove creeks.With dense mangrove forest on both the side,creating a natural passage.

    After visting Limestone caves, the boats will bring you back to Baratang.Board our cabs and return to Port blair.overnight stay at Port Blair.

  • Day 3: Port Blair - Ross Island - North Bay (Coral Island) - Port Blair

    After breakfast early,our team takes you to Water sports complex.From here we start for a Full-day boat excursion to North Bay (Coral Island) & Ross Island.

    First we head to North bay island to see coral reefs,exotic varieties of beautiful ornamental fishes and other marine life.You can enjoy glass bottom boat ride,snorkeling and other water sports activities like Jet Ski Ride, Sea walk etc (Optional).

    In the afternoon,we visit Ross Island, from here the British governed the entire Andaman & Nicobar Islands, prior to India’s Independence.There is also a cemetery and a small museum managed by the Indian Navy.This Island is an ideal destination for nature walk amidst sylvan surroundings with deer,peacocks and other exotic birds.

    After exploring Ross island,we head back to Port Blair.Upon arrival our cabs take you back to your resort.Overnight stay at Port Blair.

  • Day 4: Port Blair - Havelock – Radhanagar Beach

    After breakfast and checkout, our team member will take you to Haddo jetty by Cab,board the inter-island cruise to Havelock Island (57 kms by sea from Port Blair).

    Havelock is a picturesque natural paradise with beautiful white sandy beaches, rich coral reefs and lush green forest. It is one of the populated islands in the Andaman group of Island,with an area of 113 sq. km.On Arrival at Havelock Jetty, our team will assist you to board the AC Cab waiting to transfer you to the hotel.

    After lunch, board our Cabs and visit the World Famous “Radha Nagar Beach” situated on the western coast of Havelock Island,it is one of the most popular destinations for tourists in Andaman Islands.With its breathtaking sunsets,this beach is nothing less but a tropical heaven.Pristine sand,and the clear emerald blue waves and a lot of peace and serenity are the hallmark of this beautiful paradise.Witness the famous Sunset from Radhanagar beach and head back to your resort .Overnight at Havelock.

  • Day 5: Havelock - Kalapathar Beach & Elephanta Beach

    After breakfast,we head to Elephanta Beach,the ancient secluded beach.Elephanta beach is famous for all kind of water sports.Rest of the day is dedicated to your love of the ocean and associated water sports like scuba ,snorkeling,para sailing etc.

    Get charmed by the sugary white sand and the contrasting shadows of the old trees at the beach, which creates an illusionistic picturesque to mesmerise you. Spend the day snorkelling in the water and take the exotic view of the corals and the marine life under the sea, at Elephanta beach.

    In the evening,we proceed to Kala Pathar Beach.The beach gets its name from the surrounding village,and the black rocks that adorn the coastline here.Silvery sand and aqua green waters make the place exotic and exquisite and make this beach a great place to relax.It is a long stretch of white silky sand with big black rocks beside the blue sea and it is situated on one corner of Havelock Island.

    In the evening we return to our resort. Overnight stay at Havelock.

  • Day 6: Havelock – Port Blair

    After breakfast checkout from your resort, our team will escort you to Havelock jetty and help you board the cruise which will sail to Port Blair.Back at Port Blair we escort you to your resort and help you check in.Overnight stay at Port Blair.

  • Day 7: Departure

    After breakfast, checkout from the hotel, and our cab will be waiting outside to take you to the Airport.
